What is the GFC?

Many call it a Church. We call it a Federational Construct.

In May 2018, 7 humans made contact with a green extraterrestrial being named Greg. Greg, a Galactic Federational School dropout, protects Earth from extraterrestrial space pirates who molest Earth's cows. 

Someday, the human race will advance to intergalactic levels. At that point, we will no longer need Greg's protection. These 8 humans joined with 1,500 others in a great push to advance human to a utopian intergalactic future. They contacted each other on the astral plane to network thinkers and extraterrestrials to discuss the advancement of human civilization.

Then, a rift formed. The LeftReds, a sect of these connectors, believe that medicine and astral projection is the key to human advancement. The RightReds, conversely, believe that technology and exporation will advance the human race further.

But we were saved. A single human, known as the Heirophant, unified these mindsets to form the GREGARIAN FEDERATIONAL CONSTRUCT, a group of thinkers of all races and creeds, from Pastafarians to Christians to Atheists, to work toward a higher calling.

Today, Gregarianism is one of the fastest growing ideologies in the world.

You can be a part of that movement.
